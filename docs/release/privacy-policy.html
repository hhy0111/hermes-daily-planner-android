Privacy Policy

Hermes Daily Planner
Effective date: June 20, 2026

Hermes Daily Planner is a local-first Android planner for tasks, schedules, notes, language settings, and reminders. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app stores, how it is used, and how advertising works in the app.

Data Stored On The Device

The app stores planner data locally on the user's device, including:

  • Tasks and task details
  • Notes
  • Schedules and reminder times
  • App language preference

This data is stored in app-local storage and is used to provide the app's core planner features.

Data Collection

The current release does not require an account and does not send planner data such as tasks, notes, or schedules to a remote server operated by the developer.

The current release includes the Google Mobile Ads SDK to show banner ads. It does not include analytics SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, or cloud sync.

Permissions

The app may request notification permission so schedule reminders can be shown.

The app may request exact alarm access so reminders can ring closer to the selected time. If exact alarm access is not granted, the app may use Android's inexact alarm behavior.

The app may receive shared text, image, or PDF content when the user explicitly chooses Hermes Daily Planner from Android's share sheet.

Advertising

The current release uses Google AdMob banner ads.

Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect or share data such as IP address, user product interactions, diagnostic information, and device or account identifiers for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention. The Google Play Data safety form must be completed based on the active SDK version and app configuration before submission.

Learn more about Google's advertising data practices at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Data Sharing

The current release does not share planner content such as tasks, notes, or schedules with third parties.

The Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect or share advertising-related device and interaction data as described by Google's SDK disclosure documentation.

If the user exports or shares data manually, the destination is chosen by the user and is outside the app's control.

Data Deletion

Users can delete app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage through Android system settings. If in-app deletion controls are added, this section should be updated.

Children

The app is intended for general productivity and study planning. If the app is marketed to children or selected for child-directed target audiences in Google Play, this policy and app behavior must be reviewed for child privacy requirements before release.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact privacy@hermesdailyplanner.app.

Changes

This policy may be updated when app features, data practices, advertising, analytics, or sync behavior change.
